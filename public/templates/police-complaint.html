Incident Details

Signature of Complainant
Important Legal Information: Under Section 154 of CrPC, the police are legally obligated to register an FIR for cognizable offenses. If the police refuse to register your complaint, you may approach the Superintendent of Police or file a complaint before the Magistrate under Section 156(3) of CrPC. It is advisable to file complaints promptly after the incident occurs.
